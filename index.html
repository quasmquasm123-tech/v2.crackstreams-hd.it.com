Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to use free sports streaming sites?

Streaming from unofficial sources exists in a legal grey area. While watching is generally not illegal for end-users in most countries, distributing copyrighted content without permission is illegal. Always check your local laws.

Why do streaming sites get taken down?

Sports leagues and broadcasters aggressively protect their broadcasting rights. They issue DMCA takedown notices and work with ISPs to block access to infringing sites.

Do I need to register or pay for streams?

Legitimate free streaming sites never require registration or payment. If a site asks for credit card information or personal details, it's likely a scam.

How can I find working stream links?

Check Reddit communities like r/nflstreams (now closed) or r/mmastreams. Aggregator sites like ours constantly update working links as old ones get taken down.
