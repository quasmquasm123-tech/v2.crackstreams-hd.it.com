Digital Millennium Copyright Act Compliance & Takedown Procedures

DMCA Compliance Statement

LiveSports (CrackStreams alternatives aggregator) respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We promptly respond to notices of alleged copyright infringement.

Important Notice

We are a link aggregation service. We do NOT host any streams or copyrighted content on our servers. We provide links to third-party streaming sites. However, we will promptly remove links to infringing content upon proper DMCA notification.

DMCA Takedown Procedure

If you are a copyright owner or authorized agent and believe your copyrighted work is being linked to without authorization, please follow this procedure:

Step 1: Send a Proper DMCA Notice

Your DMCA notice must include:

  • Identification: Detailed description of the copyrighted work
  • Infringing Material: Specific URLs linking to infringing content
  • Contact Information: Your name, address, phone, email
  • Good Faith Statement: Statement of good faith belief
  • Accuracy Statement: Statement under penalty of perjury
  • Signature: Physical or electronic signature

Step 2: Our Response

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:

  • We will acknowledge receipt within 24-48 hours
  • We will remove or disable access to the infringing links
  • We will notify the affected user (if applicable)
  • We maintain records of all takedown notices

Response Time

We typically respond to valid DMCA notices within 24-48 hours. Please allow up to 3 business days for complete processing.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification:

Counter-Notification Requirements

Your counter-notification must include:

  • Identification of the removed material
  • Statement under penalty of perjury of good faith belief
  • Your name, address, phone number
  • Consent to jurisdiction in your district
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Important: We will forward counter-notifications to the original complainant. If they don't file a lawsuit within 14 business days, we may restore the removed material.

Repeat Infringer Policy

We maintain a repeat infringer policy in accordance with DMCA requirements:

  • We track DMCA notices associated with users
  • Users who receive multiple valid notices may be banned
  • We terminate accounts of repeat infringers
  • We maintain records for legal compliance
